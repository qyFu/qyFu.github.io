Caged Bird

BY MAYA ANGELOU

A free bird leaps
on the back of the wind
and floats downstream
till the current ends
and dips his wing
in the orange sun rays
and dares to claim the sky.

But a bird that stalks
down his narrow cage
can seldom see through
his bars of rage
his wings are clipped and
his feet are tied
so he opens his throat to sing.

The caged bird sings
with a fearful trill
of things unknown
but longed for still
and his tune is heard
on the distant hill
for the caged bird
sings of freedom.

The free bird thinks of another breeze
and the trade winds soft through the sighing trees
and the fat worms waiting on a dawn bright lawn
and he names the sky his own

But a caged bird stands on the grave of dreams
his shadow shouts on a nightmare scream
his wings are clipped and his feet are tied
so he opens his throat to sing.

The caged bird sings
with a fearful trill
of things unknown
but longed for still
and his tune is heard
on the distant hill
for the caged bird
sings of freedom.

Welcome to the GISAXS Community Website. This is a place for the community of GISAXS and GIWAXS researchers (instrument scientists and users), and for the x-ray/neutron scattering communities more broadly.

The intention is to provide:

Tutorials to help new users get accustomed to GISAXS experiments and data analysis.
Technical articles on various aspects of scattering theory and experiment.
Information on the capabilities of synchrotron beamlines (and how to get access).
A way for the community to interact, share news, and help one another.
